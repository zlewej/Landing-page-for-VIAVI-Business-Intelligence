What is OBIEE:

OBIEE- Oracle Business Intelligence Enterprise Edition is a comprehensive business intelligence and analytics platform that provides a foundation for building enterprise BI solutions, including range of end user tools, such as interactive dashboards, ad hoc queries, notifications and alerts for the scheduled jobs. It is known for performing actionable data analysis and removing redundancy in report creation.

What is a Dashboard:

Is a powerful, easy-to-use, Web based reports that provides timely, accurate and actionable reporting for business.

What is a Report:

A Dashboard is made of multiple pages that contains targeted and focused data in a structured manner. Within Dashboards there are multiple page and each page will have multiple reports.

What is a Prompt/Filter:

It is a filter by which the data can be sliced and diced in many ways. Prompts play a pivotal role in providing fast, accurate and actionable reports. Prompts can be applied across Dashboard or can be specific to one report.
